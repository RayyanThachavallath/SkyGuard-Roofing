ABOUT US

Our Story, Your Shelter.

Founded in 2015 and headquartered in Kochi, Kerala, SkyGuard Roofing has grown into one of the region’s most trusted names in roofing and waterproofing services. We specialize in residential and commercial roofing, industrial roof installations, emergency leak repairs, and preventive maintenance — all built to handle Kerala’s harsh monsoon climate and intense heat.

What sets us apart is our commitment to quality craftsmanship, transparent communication, and customer-first service. Whether it’s a full roof installation or a small repair job, we approach every project with the same level of care and professionalism. Our team of experienced engineers and technicians work with top-grade materials, modern equipment, and proven methods to ensure long-lasting results.

At SkyGuard, we believe that a roof is more than just shelter — it’s protection for your home, your business, and your peace of mind. That’s why we offer customized roofing solutions for every client — from individual homeowners and apartment associations to construction companies and educational institutions.

With hundreds of successful projects and satisfied clients across Kerala, SkyGuard Roofing continues to raise the standard in durability, safety, and trust. We don’t just install roofs — we build relationships that last.

Our Mission

At SkyGuard Roofing Solutions, our mission is to redefine the roofing industry in Kerala by delivering superior craftsmanship, innovative solutions, and unwavering reliability. We aim to provide every homeowner and business with roofing systems that not only protect but also enhance the beauty and value of their property. By combining top-quality materials, skilled professionals, and a commitment to eco-friendly practices, we ensure that every project stands the test of time. Our customer-first approach means we listen carefully, communicate transparently, and deliver results that exceed expectations — because to us, it’s not just about building roofs; it’s about building trust, safety, and lasting relationships in every corner we cover.
